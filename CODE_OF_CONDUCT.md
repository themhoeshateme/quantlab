# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in QuantNova a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or location.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its contributors.
- Ask questions when requirements are unclear.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or repeated disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Sharing secrets, tokens, or credentials
- Any behavior that would reasonably make contributors feel unsafe or unwelcome

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report concerns through the maintainer contact channel listed in the README. A dedicated contact method should be added before the project is widely promoted.
